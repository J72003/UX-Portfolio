About

Researcher · CS '26 · San Antonio

I'm a UX researcher and product designer who treats research as the foundation of design, not the prelude to it.

I'm a Computer Science senior at Texas A&M University–San Antonio, currently designing for the SMB digital banking portfolio at Frost Bank. My work moves between research, interaction design, and Agile delivery — which means I'm comfortable writing user stories, leading refinement sessions, and presenting to product and business stakeholders in the same week. Banking has real complexity: permissions, approvals, fraud controls, compliance. The interesting design problem isn't removing that complexity. It's making it feel simple to the people who have to live with it.

I treat accessibility as part of research, not as a checklist at the end. For government and regulated work specifically, that means designing research participation, not just outcomes — recruiting inclusively, accommodating assistive technology, and writing in plain language. I'm continuing to build my familiarity with WCAG and Section 508 standards as I move toward more public-facing work.

Outside of UX, I'm usually reading, taking photos, studying product details, or overthinking how to write a cleaner email.
