What I do

Product analysis, discovery, and requirements
Product Discovery

I investigate ambiguous product questions before teams commit to a direction. At Frost I turned "which features do SMBs want?" into "which workflow does this business run?" Two very different questions with very different product answers.

Customer & Market Research

I build discussion guides, participant screeners, and behavior-focused research protocols; run structured interviews and UserTesting studies; benchmark competitors; and synthesize findings across 12+ customer sessions and 13+ platform audits.

Product Metrics

I define north star metrics, supporting KPIs, and counter-metrics framed around testable product hypotheses. Across Frost, PortrAid, and InverteQuest, each metric set is tied to a specific decision the team would need to make.

Requirements Definition

I translate synthesis into user stories, acceptance criteria, and product recommendations. At Frost, research findings moved from a readout into backlog refinement in the same week, with requirements adopted into sprint planning.

AI Product Analysis

I evaluate how model performance, confidence scores, failure modes, and uncertainty should shape the product experience. At PortrAid and InverteQuest, the core question was the same: when an AI gives a prediction, how should the product help users decide what to do next?

Technical Fluency

My CS background lets me work across product, design, and engineering without translation. I understand precision, recall, F1, and confidence variance; I can write SQL, read APIs, and evaluate implementation tradeoffs in product conversations.
